Fuelled UK — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Fuelled UK ("the App") is developed by David Picknell. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App on iPhone.

Privacy first: Fuelled UK stores your data on your device and in your private iCloud account. Your data is never shared with third parties or used for advertising.

Data You Provide

Fuelled UK stores the fuel entries and vehicle details you create, including vehicle names, fuel type, odometer readings, litres purchased, and prices paid.

This data is stored locally on your device and synced to your private iCloud account (via CloudKit) so it is available across your devices.

Your data is not accessible to the developer, not shared with third parties, and not used for advertising.

iCloud Sync

Fuelled UK uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your vehicles and fuel entries across devices signed into the same Apple ID. This data is stored in your private iCloud database.

The developer does not have access to your iCloud data. Apple's handling of iCloud data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Collection

Fuelled UK does not collect personal data. No names, email addresses, device identifiers, or usage analytics are collected by the developer.

Local Processing

All MPG calculations, cost-per-litre calculations, and chart data are processed locally on your device.

No external servers are used beyond Apple's iCloud infrastructure.

Analytics & Tracking

Fuelled UK does not use analytics services, advertising frameworks, tracking technologies, or third-party SDKs.

No behavioural data, crash analytics, or usage statistics are collected.

In-App Purchase

Fuelled UK includes a seven-day free trial period. After the trial ends, a one-time lifetime purchase is required to unlock continued use. There are no subscriptions.

All payments are processed securely by Apple through Apple's In-App Purchase system. The developer does not receive or store your payment information.

Apple's handling of payment information is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual vehicles and their associated fuel history from within the App. A "Delete All Data" option in Settings permanently removes all data from your device and iCloud. Deleted data cannot be recovered.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. No accounts or personal identification are required to use the App.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
