1. Description of the App

Fuelled UK is a fuel tracking application for iPhone designed for UK drivers. It allows you to log fuel fill-ups across multiple vehicles and monitor fuel efficiency and spending over time.

Key features include:

  • Multi-vehicle fuel logging (Petrol and Diesel)
  • Automatic MPG and cost-per-litre calculations
  • MPG and cost-per-litre charts with trend lines
  • Fill-up history with edit and delete
  • MOT, tax, and insurance expiry tracking with optional reminders
  • iCloud sync across devices via CloudKit

The App works without requiring an internet connection. All calculations are performed locally on your device. Data syncs to iCloud when a connection is available.

2. Accuracy and Limitations
